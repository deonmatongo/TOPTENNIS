## **How I Actually Use This**

### **Scenario 1: Scheduling a Match**

Let's say I need to play against Sarah Chen:

1. I go to **Division Standings** tab
2. Find Sarah in the list
3. Click the **"Schedule"** button next to her name
4. A notification pops up: "Opening Sarah Chen's availability calendar..."
5. I'm taken to her calendar page with an orange banner saying "League Match Scheduling"
6. I see all her available time slots
7. I pick a time that works for me (like "Dec 22, 7:00 PM")
8. Click **"Request League Match"**
9. Done! She gets a notification and can accept or suggest another time

### **Scenario 2: After Playing a Match**

I just finished playing Mike Johnson and won 6-4, 7-5:

1. Go to **Matches** tab
2. Find the match with Mike
3. Click **"Report Score"**
4. A popup appears where I enter the scores:
   - Set 1: 6-4
   - Set 2: 7-5
5. Click Submit
6. Boom! The leaderboard updates automatically
7. My points go up
8. My rank might change
9. Everyone in the division sees the updated standings instantly

### **Scenario 3: Checking My Progress**

I want to see if I'm playoff eligible:

1. Go to **Division Standings** tab
2. Look at my row
3. If it says "Playoff Ready" with a green badge - I'm in! üéâ
4. If it says "2 more needed" - I need to play 2 more matches to qualify

## **The Smart Stuff That Happens Behind the Scenes**

### **Automatic Updates**

When someone else reports a score or schedules a match, I don't need to refresh the page - it updates automatically! It's like magic but it's actually real-time technology.

### **Smart Grouping**

When I first joined the league, the system automatically put me in a division with players at my skill level. So I'm not playing against pros when I'm intermediate - everyone in my division is roughly the same level.

### **Points System**

Every time I win, I get 3 points. The more matches I play and win, the higher I climb. The system calculates everything automatically - I just play tennis and watch my rank go up!

## **Common Questions**

### **Q: How do I join a league?**
A: Look for the "Browse Available Leagues" button or check the Leagues section of the app.

### **Q: Can I be in multiple leagues at once?**
A: Yes! You can join as many leagues as you want. Each will show up as a separate card in My Leagues.

### **Q: What if my opponent doesn't show up?**
A: You can report this through the match details. The system will handle forfeit rules.

### **Q: How are divisions determined?**
A: The system automatically groups you with players of similar skill level based on your profile.

### **Q: Can I see other divisions?**
A: No, for privacy and fair play, you can only see your own division's details.

### **Q: What happens if I lose a match?**
A: Your record updates, but you don't lose points - you just don't gain any. Keep playing to improve your ranking!

### **Q: When do playoffs start?**
A: This depends on the league schedule. Check with your league administrator or watch for announcements.
